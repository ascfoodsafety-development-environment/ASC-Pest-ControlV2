Head Office Region

Gqeberha (Port Elizabeth) & Nelson Mandela Bay

Our head office is based in Gqeberha, giving us the fastest response times across Nelson Mandela Bay. We service residential suburbs, industrial zones, Coega IDZ, the harbour precinct and the full metro area — covering both residential and commercial clients with the complete range of ASC services. Being locally based means we can respond to urgent infestations on the same day, and our knowledge of the city's pest pressures and seasonal activity patterns makes our programmes more targeted and effective.
